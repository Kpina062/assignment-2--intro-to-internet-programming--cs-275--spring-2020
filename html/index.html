Rice Recipes

Making Sprouted California Rice

For slightly al dente rice: Combine 10 ounces of rice with 16 ounces of water or broth and 1 Tbsp olive oil. Bring to a boil and stir once to mix. Reduce heat to low, cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook for 25 minutes. Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and serve.

For softer rice: Increase liquid by 4 ounces and cook time by 5 minutes.

Making White Rice

Combine 8 ounces of rice with 16 ounces of water and 1 Tbsp olive oil. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to the lowest setting. Cook for about 18 minutes.
